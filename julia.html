Hi, my name is Daphine Nambafu and am very excited to share with you my experience about the Leadership and Personal Development.

it was an infirmative class I should say

Well, this has been very educative, I won’t say that have not a lesson or lecture like this in my life but this has been different. I feel that have improved in the areas that have been badly off, the self-checks just hit differently and in a positive way that they just get me into a position of understanding myself better, my weaknesses and strengths. I believe have improved and started applying what have learnt in my life.

Being proactive

I learnt that being proactive makes me feel better and it creates space for effective thinking. It allows me see positivity, this spoke to me and I am working on being more proactive than reactive because being reactive limits me from thinking creatively.

Beginning with an end in mind

Here what worked for was the paradigm that am supposed to clearly define my vision and purpose in life and this is going to make a difference in my life.

Putting first things first

I learnt that am supposed to I should spend my time on what’s important, focus on my highest priorities, eliminate the un important, plan my every week and stay true in the moment of choice. And I considered planning every week as one that is going to help me develop personally and in this, I learnt that I plan my week by connecting with my mission, roles and goals, scheduling the big rocks and lastly scheduling my activities around the big rocks.

Thinking win-win

Having an abundance mentality, balancing courage and consideration, considering other people’s wins as well as your own and creating win-win agreements. With courage I am supposed to share my ideas and opinions with confidence, with consideration I am supposed to acknowledge other people’s ideas and opinions, these stood out for me.

Seeking first to understand then to be understood

Here I learnt that I have greater influence with others if I truly understand them first and understanding precedes action and judgement. What worked out for me was empathic listening, that my intention as am listening must be I listen to understand and reflect feelings and words, with this am supposed to ask clarifying questions.

Involving actively collaborating with others

Here I learnt that its creative cooperation amongst teams, groups to achieve a planned/set goal. So, we are meant to come up with something that’s better than what either of us have in mind. I have to value differences and seek the third alternatives.

Investing in myslef comes first

This is the most important habit because it covers all the other six, it’s the one that helps us deal with the rest because it deals with me my body, heart, mind and spirit, it shows me that its me to invest in myself before investing in others. I must care for my body, spirit, mind and heart.

Am grateful, discoverd myself more

Well, my feeling in this class, I felt like have discovered me more, how I should run myself in order to be effective and efficient in all spheres of my life. From the level of dependency (attitude of you) to independency (attitude of I) to interdependency (attitude of we). Am just too grateful that am equipped I should say and these classes have been very helpful. Thank you Julia and Michael your impacting lives.
